Simple Garlic Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

200 g pasta
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
Salt and black pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
A handful of fresh basil
30 g grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
Add the cherry tomatoes, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 5–7 minutes until the tomatoes become soft.
Drain the pasta and add it to the pan. Mix everything well.
Add fresh basil and Parmesan cheese on top.
Serve warm.

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients

200 g pasta
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
150 g mushrooms, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
150 ml cooking cream
Salt and black pepper
30 g grated Parmesan cheese
Fresh parsley for serving

Instructions

Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add the onion and cook for 2–3 minutes until soft.
Add the mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
Pour in the cooking cream and stir well.
Add salt, black pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
Drain the pasta and mix it into the sauce.
Serve with fresh parsley on top.

Tip: You can add a little pasta water to make the sauce extra smooth.
